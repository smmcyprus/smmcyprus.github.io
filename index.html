ABOUT

Tziortzis was born in 1997 and he is the
only single seater driver in Cyprus at present. With a Russian mother and a Cypriot father he is currently based in Cyprus.

Last year he drove for British Team - Fortec Motorsports and in 2017 for the Russian leading team SMP Racing, showing brilliant performance in both the Formula Renault 2.0 EuroCup and the SMP F4 North Europe Zone Championship Certified by FIA.

Tziortzis stood out early, in Cyprus and abroad, with various accomplishments in Karting. He has since driven a Formula 4 and a Formula 3 car in major European circuits like Monte Carlo, Monza, Silverstone and many others, recording impressive results.

Tziortzis was the Cypriot Karting Champion for seven years, attracting the interest of various teams.
