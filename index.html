ABOUT

Tziortzis was born in 1997 and he is the
only single seater driver in Cyprus at present. With a Russian mother and a Cypriot father he is currently based in Cyprus.

Tziortzis stood out early, in Cyprus and abroad, with various acomplishments in Karting. He was the Cypriot Karting Champion for seven years, attracting the interest of various teams. He has since driven a Formula 4 and a Formula 3 car in major Worldwide circuits like Monte Carlo, Abu Dhabi, Monza, Silverstone and many others, recording impressive results.

In 2017 he drove for the Russian leading team SMP Racing, showing brilliant performance in SMP F4 North Europe Zone Championship Certified by FIA. He has attained 3 podiums in Moscow Raceway and was also chosen as the Best Spirit Driver for the 2017 season. Last year he participated in the Formula Renault Eurocup with the British Team - Fortec Motorsports. 

In 2019 he participated in the official tests for the Formula Renault Eurocup in Yas Marina Circuit in Abu Dhabi, Paul Ricard Circuit in France, Cremona Circuit in Italy with the Italian team - JD Motorsports as a preparation for the 2020 racing season.
